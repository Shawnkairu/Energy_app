How it works

From rooftop to resident, in six steps.

01

Measure

Data is captured across production, consumption, and the grid.

02

Ingest

e.mappa ingests and validates every reading in real time.

03

Allocate

Tokens are assigned fairly and transparently to residents.

04

Visualize

Stakeholders view live data via apps and dashboards.

05

Settle

Payments are settled and payouts are automated.

06

Certify

Partners follow certified workflows and strict standards.

A trusted, transparent, repeatable energy economy.
