# Settlement Rules

Settlement is based on monetized prepaid solar only.

## Non-Negotiables

- No prepaid cash means no solar allocation and no stakeholder payout.
- Generated but unused energy does not create payout.
- Wasted, curtailed, or free-exported energy does not create payout.
- Ownership percentages control future cashflows.
- Share transfers affect future settlement periods only after payment clears and compliance checks pass.

## Waterfall

Revenue is allocated in this order:

1. Reserve
2. Providers
3. Financiers
4. Building owner
5. e.mappa

## Ownership

Provider owns 100% initially. If residents buy 30%, provider retains 70% of future provider-side payouts and residents receive 30%. The system must never double count sold ownership.

## Financier Recovery

Financiers are deal-based. They receive project-specific payouts during the recovery phase, then may step down to a royalty phase. There is no opaque pooled fund in the initial product.
